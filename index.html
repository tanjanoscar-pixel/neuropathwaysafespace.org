About NeuroPathway

NeuroPathway is a unified digital ecosystem designed to transform early identification, reduce crisis, and create a connected support pathway for children, young people, and adults.

It combines two integrated platforms into a real-time early-warning, early-help, and evidence-generation system that supports wellbeing, reduces exclusions, accelerates EHCP processes, and prevents late-stage crises that burden the NHS and councils.

The Problem

Across the UK, children and young people are being identified far too late. Families fall through the gaps. Professionals miss early signs. Children enter crisis before anyone connects the dots.

The Solution

A single connected ecosystem, replacing siloed tools and paperwork with a digital, real-time, multi-agency early-identification system.

Benefits & Impact

For Children & Young People

  • Earlier recognition of needs
  • Reduced anxiety, crisis, and isolation
  • Safer, supported transitions
  • A voice in their own journey

For Schools

  • 40–60% reduction in EHCP evidence time
  • Consistent, high-quality documentation
  • Fewer exclusions (saving £18k–£36k per pupil/year)
  • Clear patterns and triggers

For NHS & CAMHS

  • Early triage insights before crisis
  • Reduced repeat assessments
  • Better handovers
  • Fewer A&E mental health presentations

For Local Authorities

  • Better early help targeting
  • Fewer late-stage safeguarding escalations
  • Stronger multi-agency communication
  • SEND reform compliance
